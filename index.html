Chocolate Cake in a Mug Recipe

Enjoy a quick dessert in minutes!

Prep Time: 5 min // Cook Time: 2 min // Total Time: 7 min

What You'll Need:

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 2 tbs unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp baking soda
  • 3 tbs milk
  • 2 tbs canola oil
  • 1 tbs water
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream (optional)

Directions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt; mix until evenly incorporated.
  2. Add in milk, canola oil, water, and vanilla extract into dry mixture; mix until evenly incorporated.
  3. Cook batter in microwave for 1 min 45 sec at 1000 watts or until cake is done in the middle.
  4. Let cake cool for 3 min.
  5. Add vanilla ice cream if desired; enjoy in mug!
